Artifact

Digital artefact creation.

Submit your individual analysis of your own process of decision and creation of your digital object/ artefact on your website 250 words (approx).

Upload the URL that links directly to your analysis here then describe your process in the:

Research - why did you decide to build this on this topic, with this content?

Design - how did you agree/design what it would be - for example, why text and not audio, why a timeline rather than a video, why a map?

Implementation - how did it work - how successful is it in achieving your initial goal?

Reflection - what worked well? What would you do differently if anything?
